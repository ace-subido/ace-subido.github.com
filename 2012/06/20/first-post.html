Under construction

Getting back into writing with a renewed focus. I’m giving my blog a fresh new restart from theperiva.tumblr.com (though I won’t retire that blog, it’ll be archived). theperiva was a bit random, it was literally a notepad of scribbles from me. This site will be a reboot of that, I’ll be focusing more on this being a journal rather than a repository of random finds :)

Why tumblr?

Tumblr is the nearest next thing to the most stripped down blogging engine I know. It has no army of settings to tinker with, or a plethora of css files to deal with. It simply allows me to have this quiet blue space to write, just to concentrate on producing content.

Hopefully this’ll be finished before the weekend. Excited to write about a lot of things in this space of mine!
