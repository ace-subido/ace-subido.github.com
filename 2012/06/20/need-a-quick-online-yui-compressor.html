Need a quick online YUI compressor?

Need a quick online YUI compressor?

Online YUI Compressor: refresh-sf.com

Whenever I wear my “client-side engineer” hat, I always have this tool in my tool belt. It’s an easy-to-use service to compress Javascript and CSS files. If your CSS is using LESS you’re better off with SimpLESS. Other than that, this online YUI compressor does a fantastic job.
