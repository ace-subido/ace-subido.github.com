Our RSVP page for our Febraury 2 Wedding is currently under construction :) Here’s the process I went through

  • Hand-rolled the responsive layout with Bootstrap
  • Get the form in Google Docs so that all entries get loaded into our Excel Sheet :D
  • Used Tumblr to host the form itself.

All in all I got it up and running between 50 minutes of actual work, most of the time I waited for the DNS to kick-in since we got a subdomain for that. With my schedule, that 50 minutes was spread across 2 days :))

Still finalizing the overall tone with Elain though :D I’m excited for the wedding!
