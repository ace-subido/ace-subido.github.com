Corporate Services

Our qualified lawyers, accountants and company secretaries, all with extensive experience and broad commercial knowledge in Asia, provide creative, practical and cost-effective solutions to complex business situations. Complete confi dentiality is assured.

How we do it

At every stage of setting up, building or winding up a company, there are many factors you need to consider, including your set-up and maintenance costs, licences, tax efficiency, the political stability of the places in which you do business, the disclosure and other regulatory requirements of each jurisdiction. We’ll help you handle all these complex issues, so you can focus on your core business tasks.

Our services include:

  • Forming or acquiring Hong Kong and offshore companies (BVI, Samoa, Seychelles, Caymans, Panama, Liberia, Delaware, etc.)Designing holding structures to reflect administrative requirements and to maximise tax efficiency
  • Corporate secretarial services
  • Custodial and escrow services
  • Advising on taxation issues and management, ownership and control
  • Establishing businesses and joint ventures in China and advising on cross-border investments for China, India etc.
  • Ensuring timely compliance with accounting and auditing requirements
  • Registering branch offices of foreign companies in Hong Kong
  • Effecting de-registration or dormancy for Hong Kong companies and winding-up or re-instating offshore companies
  • Arranging domicile migration for offshore companies
  • Assisting with joint ventures, mergers, acquisitions and re-organisations
  • Liaising with banks, brokers, auditors and other professional advisors
  • Virtual office services
  • Assist in opening bank accounts for Hong Kong and offshore companies
  • Corporate nominee shareholders and nominee directors
