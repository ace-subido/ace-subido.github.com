Our Firm

AFP Group was established in 2004, bringing together like-minded, multi skilled professionals with international training and experience and particular expertise in doing business in Asia. In everything we do, our aim is to help enterprising people create, grow, manage, and protect their wealth.

All too often I have seen business structures that are inappropriate, too complex and very costly to maintain. As independent advisors we are able to avoid conflicts of interest and focus on what is right for the client. How do we do that? By ensuring that we have exceptional people who listen to clients, understand their issues, and provide confidential, ethical and appropriate advice and support. Our team includes experts in the fields of accounting, finance, tax law, corporate services, trusts and trust administration.

Enterprising individuals, entrepreneurs, business-owners and families come to us for many reasons – perhaps they want to create a new business or establish a family trust, expand their business in another country, structure their business and personal financial affairs to minimise tax obligations and maximise their wealth.

We handle the complex issues, the detailed paperwork and the day-to-day management, so that clients can get on with doing what they do best, reaping the rewards, supporting the causes they believe in, and building a meaningful legacy.

We also work closely with intermediaries such as banks, asset managers, law firms and accounting firms in order to provide specialist services to their clients.

Roddy Sage
Founder and Chairman, AFP Group
