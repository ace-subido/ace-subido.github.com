China Market Entry

Given the administrative regulations and bureaucratic problems that are firmly attached to setting up business in China, AFP is well placed to provide all your requirements, to the point that you need not even make a visit, if you wish, until the time your business commences operations.

Utilizing its well-connected team of professionals, AFP takes on these responsibilities to simplify the process, efficiently completing all company registration documentation and industry sector applications, and obtaining government licenses to allow your new business to be properly established in the shortest possible timeframe.

Our services for your business in China include:

A. Onshore Company Registration for Wholly Foreign-owned Enterprise, Joint Venture and Representative Office

  • End to end Incorporation Service
  • Name registration approval & Industrial Pre-approval
  • Certificate of Approval
  • Business License
  • Company Chops Engraving
  • Enterprise Code
  • Foreign Exchange Registration
  • Tax Registration
  • Bank Account Opening
  • Capital Verification
  • Labor Registration
  • Financial Registration
  • Statistics Registration
  • Customs Registration
  • Licenses update after the initial capital verification
  • Coordinate other issues in relation to the project
  • Restructuring existing companies and foreign representative offices including: change of name, shareholders, directors, business scope, registered address and registered capital and investment.


B. Onshore Accounting, Tax & Payroll Services

  • Accounts setting up
  • Accounting software section
  • Tax model and invoices application
  • Bookkeeping
  • Expenses report (for ROs) or monthly or yearly financial statements (for JVs and WOFEs)
  • Tax computerization and filing
  • Audit coordination
  • Payroll services
  • Accountants recruitment and training
