Wealth Management

Switzerland remains the centre of Europe’s wealth management industry. And AFP’s associate, Allied Finance of Vaduz, Liechtenstein, carries an impressive portfolio throughout Europe and Asia.

Through this solid partnership, AFP offers wealth management services that include:

  • performance monitoring system for fund managers
  • maintain books and records for clients and undertake all regulatory and tax filings as appropriate
  • options from a wide range of investment advisory services, including the selection of private banks and asset managers, facilitating the opening of bank accounts and the acquisition of assets throughout Europe and USA.
