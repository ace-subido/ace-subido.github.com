Learning new things together at the 24/7 CodeCamp

The 24/7 CodeCamp is a non-competitive collaborative learning meetup for 24/7 International Inc., resource partners. It's a venue for a fun, interesting and exciting learning experience for everyone.

CodeCamps bring the community together to create new work relationships and to make them better at their respective crafts so that they can continue providing value to people.
