In your life, you will encounter people who are talented and gifted, when they speak you will be wow'ed, they’ll talk the talk. Do not be wow'ed by these people, instead look at their fruit. A tree does not strive to bear fruit, it produces naturally over a large amount of time, nothing flashy or fancy, it’s authentic and genuine. Fruitfulness is absolutely more astonishing than giftedness.
—Carlos Miguel Antonio
